Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pinard ("the app") handles information when you use the iOS app.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly in the app, such as wine cellar entries and app settings.

We may also collect basic technical data needed to operate and improve the app, such as app version, device type, and crash reports.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide, maintain, and improve the app.

We may use technical data to diagnose errors, fix bugs, and improve performance.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers that help us run the app (for example, hosting or analytics providers), only as needed for app operations.

Data Retention

We keep data only for as long as necessary to provide the app and meet legal obligations.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You can stop all collection of information by uninstalling the app. You may also contact us to request deletion of data where applicable.

Contact

If you have any privacy questions, contact us at support@example.com.
